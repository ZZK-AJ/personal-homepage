  • Prepaid card trading platform and management system

    • Project Page

    A prepaid card is a kind of consumption voucher that pre-sells multiple services for a period of time in the future to customers at one time, and gives discounts or discounts on the price. Such as fitness card, beauty care card, swimming card and so on.

    Although prepaid cards are widely used, there are also problems such as merchants running away and illegal fundraising. In response to the above problems, we plan to design and implement a prepaid card trading platform and management system.

    The feature of the system is that merchants need to apply through a third party to issue a prepaid card, and the third party keeps the consumer's capital flow and guarantees the consumer's card purchase, consumption, recharge, and card return operations.
